# Support

Use GitHub Issues for public website feedback, documentation gaps, and product questions that do not include private account details.

## Good Public Issues

- A page is unclear, broken, or points to the wrong public resource.
- The product copy creates confusion about snapshot-first or live-optional modes.
- Privacy or safety messaging needs to be clearer.
- The Omoggle alternative positioning needs a factual correction.

## Do Not Post Publicly

- Camera images or private media
- Names, emails, payment details, or account-specific support requests
- Passwords, tokens, cookies, or session data
- Vulnerability details with an exploitable proof of concept

For security concerns, follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
