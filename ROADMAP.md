# SnapMog Public Roadmap

This roadmap summarizes product directions we are evaluating or actively improving for [snapmog.com](https://snapmog.com). It is not a promise of delivery dates, pricing, model access, or final feature scope.

## Now

- Improve first-run clarity for users who understand Omoggle-style camera matching.
- Keep Blind Snapshot Duel the default mode and live 1v1 clearly optional.
- Make score-first results and opponent-photo boundaries easier to understand.
- Keep independence from Omoggle visible whenever comparison language is prominent.

## Next

- Improve Solo Camera Score explanations without implying scientific or biometric assessment.
- Refine leaderboard and claimed profile messaging around entertainment-only scores.
- Clarify daily usage limits across Free, Pro, and Max plans.
- Review Live Duel Beta copy so it remains optional supporting context.

## Later

- Evaluate safer friend-challenge promotion only when product readiness supports it.
- Improve onboarding for players unfamiliar with snapshot-first duels.
- Add clearer examples of what affects camera presentation scores.
- Document safety boundaries around uploads, scoring, and other users' photos.

## Product Principles

- Do not call SnapMog official Omoggle, Omoggle 2.0, or endorsed by Omoggle.
- Do not claim scores are scientific, biometric, medical, identity, or objective attractiveness assessments.
- Do not claim snapshot scoring is no-upload or purely local.
- This repository is for public product feedback, not private production source code.
