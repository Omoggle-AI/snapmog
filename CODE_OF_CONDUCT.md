# Code of Conduct

We want discussions to stay practical, respectful, and safe for people evaluating or using SnapMog.

## Expected Behavior

- Stay on topic.
- Respect privacy and avoid posting private media or account details.
- Provide clear evidence when correcting technical or product claims.
- Keep discussions useful for new users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, sexualized comments, or personal attacks
- Posting private images, identity details, secrets, or payment data
- Spam or unrelated promotion
- Repeated off-topic arguments after maintainers ask to stop

Maintainers may edit, hide, or close content that does not fit these guidelines.
